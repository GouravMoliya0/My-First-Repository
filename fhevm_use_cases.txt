# Real-World Use Cases of Zama FHEVM

1. **Healthcare** – Encrypt patient data while allowing hospitals to compute analytics safely.  
2. **Finance** – Perform risk assessments or credit checks on encrypted financial records.  
3. **Voting Systems** – Enable private, verifiable, and secure on-chain voting.  
4. **AI Models** – Train or infer on encrypted data to preserve user privacy.  
5. **Data Marketplaces** – Sell encrypted datasets while keeping raw information hidden.

FHEVM = Privacy + Transparency + Security
