# Advantages of Using Zama FHEVM

1. **Privacy-Preserving Smart Contracts**
   - Data stays encrypted even during execution.

2. **Regulatory Compliance**
   - Helps meet data protection standards like GDPR.

3. **Secure Collaboration**
   - Multiple parties can compute on shared encrypted data.

4. **Easy Developer Integration**
   - FHEVM runs just like a normal EVM but with encryption built in.

5. **Open Source & Transparent**
   - Available on GitHub for developers to explore and contribute.
